1. What kind of fintech fields is BEA/BEAST looking for?

• We are not restricted to any fields of fintech.

• Basically, we identify and partner with companies that can provide technology and capabilities that can be integrated into the bank, furthering our product and service offerings in relevant markets.

2. How could I join BEAST?

• We welcome partners who are interested in collaborating with the bank on PoC projects to join BEAST. Their rent will be waived, and they will be offered up to HK$300,000 in PoC funding.

• Partners need to have relevant experience and demonstrate their capabilities, and their proposals should be in line with the bank's service and development strategies.

3. How long will it take for startups to join the PoC scheme offered by BEAST?

• The average PoC time is 4 months. The PoC period can be extended depending on the practical situation.

4. Will BEAST invest in startups? If so, how much capital is planned to be invested?

• This is not currently the bank's focus.

5. Will BEAST introduce startups to angel investors or venture capitalists?

• This is not currently the bank's focus.

6. What are the advantages of BEAST compared to similar programs launched by peers in Hong Kong and the Greater Bay Area? What sets BEAST apart from other fintech incubators?

• We provide funding support for PoC projects, which may be formally adopted by the bank, enabling startup ideas to be realized.

• Projects are developed jointly by startups and BEA staff, which will greatly increase the success rate.

• BEAST promotes multi-stage training within the bank to increase employees' understanding of fintech and encourages increased application.

• BEA's collaborations are led by departmental pain points, making the solutions more targeted and increasing the chances of practical applications.
