SharingBEA’s Head of Information Technology and Fintech Development Speaks at Cyberport FinTech PoC 2022

17 October 2022

The Bank of East Asia’s Head of Information Technology Department and Fintech Development Department, Mr Stephen Leung, shared about the fintech collaborations with Cyberport’s startup at Cyberport FinTech PoC Innovation Conference. BEA sponsored partnerships with Funding Reach to build solutions for enhanced credit risk management.

“Innovation is a core value at BEA. We connect with startups to co-create solutions improving operations and customer experience,” said Mr Leung. “The Cyberport FinTech PoC Scheme provides third-party validation of solutions’ potential and impact, helping us evaluate partnerships.”  

BEA worked with Funding Reach on an AI-based platform to automate corporate lending decisions and monitor credit risks. “This enhances our risk assessment and monitoring capabilities, freeing resources for high-value tasks,” Mr Leung said. BEA and Funding Reach are exploring scaling the solution for Greater Bay Area markets.

BEA shares the PoC Subsidy Scheme’s vision to support Hong Kong fintech startups. “Extra funding allows us to back more startups,” Mr Leung said. “More trials mean more learning and higher chances of success. We provide funding and share needs to help startups improve solutions benefiting us both.”

The PoC Subsidy Scheme vets solutions for Hong Kong’s banking and finance industry. For BEA, the event reinforced a vision shared with startups and stakeholders to shape financial services through partnership.
