BEAST stands for “BEA + Startups”. It is more than a co-working space which is a dedicated platform fostering innovation and collaboration between the Bank, local startups with demonstrated potential, and industry partners who can bring additional resources to support a vibrant fintech ecosystem.

Inspire

At BEAST, we strive to inspire innovative fintech collaborations and partnerships between BEA, startups, and industry partners. Through our platform, we encourage the exchange of ideas and insights, inspiring all parties to think creatively and explore new possibilities.

Co-create

We facilitate co-creation between BEA and startups at BEAST, promoting a collaborative approach to fintech development. By working together, we and our partners can develop innovative solutions tailored to the needs of our clients, resulting in more efficient and effective processes.

Support

We provide support to local startups, helping them to access to the banks and gain valuable experience. By offering a co-working space, funding support, and the opportunity to work on POC projects, we help startups to realize their ideas and gain market intelligence and feedback for further enhancement. In this way, we support the fintech community and contribute to the wider fintech ecosystem in Hong Kong.
