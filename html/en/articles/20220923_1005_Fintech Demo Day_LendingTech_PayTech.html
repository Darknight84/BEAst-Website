EventBEAST Drives Collaboration with Startups

23 September, 5 October 2022

BEAST held a Fintech Demo Day connecting 10 startups and 10 of the Bank’s business departments at the fintech co-working space. The startups pitched solutions to improve lending and cross-border payments, gaining valuable feedback and forging new partnerships.

Startups included MioTech, FreightAmigo, TranSwap, Diginex, and others. Following presentations, startups met with decision makers to discuss pilot programs and partnership opportunities.

BEAST regularly partners with accelerators Cyberport and Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation (HKSTP) to access fintech startups. BEAST thanked Cyberport and HKSTP for introducing the Bank to these innovative companies. Fintech partnerships will help BEA boost services, increase efficiency and support the local startup ecosystem.  

BEAST gives startups a platform to demonstrate solutions, gain market insights and build relationships with established players. For BEA, it means accessing cutting-edge technologies to deliver digital banking. Collaborating with accelerators and fintech startups, BEA aims to pioneer new products, improve internal processes and shape better Hong Kong’s financial services.
