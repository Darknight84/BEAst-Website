SharingBEA’s Head of Information Technology and Fintech Development Speaks at AWS Industry Forum for Financial Services

22 March 2023

The Bank of East Asia’s Head of Information Technology Department and Fintech Development Department, Mr Stephen Leung, spoke at the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Industry Forum for Financial Services in Hong Kong. In a session focused on cloud adoption in banking, Mr Stephen Leung discussed both opportunities and challenges in using cloud computing.

“BEA has been an early adopter of technologies to enhance our services, from optimizing digital platforms to collaborating with fintech startups,” Mr Leung said. “The cloud allows us to scale quickly, reduce costs and roll out new offerings for customers.”

AWS’s one-day event brought together leaders in the financial industry to explore technology themes like payments, risk management, customer experience and more. Speakers included executives from AWS, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan and others.

BEA has embarked on a digital transformation journey to becoming a “Cloud-Ready Banking Group”. By working with partners like AWS, BEA aims to gain efficiencies, upgrade legacy systems, build new cloud-native platforms and reimagine the customer experience. Cloud adoption is a key part of BEA’s vision to become a leading digital bank in Hong Kong and beyond.
