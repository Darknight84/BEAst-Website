EventBEAST Co-Hosts Cybersecurity Demo Day to Boost Digital Resilience

16 February 2023

BEAST recently co-hosted a Cybersecurity Demo Day with the Consulate General of Israel at the fintech co-working space, featuring startups with solutions to safeguarding financial services. BEA’s Head of Cybersecurity, Mr Isaiah Wong, shared how the Bank has strengthened defenses, especially for remote work.

As finance goes digital, cyber risks increase. BEA is committed to finding and piloting cutting-edge solutions that match the Bank’s dedication to innovation. By working with startups and community partners, BEA aims to defend against emerging threats.

“We thank the Consulate General of Israel for co-hosting this important session,” Mr Wong said. “Startups worldwide are pioneering technologies to build cyber resilience, and collaboration is key to implementing the best solutions.”

By forging ties with accelerators and the fintech community globally, BEA is crafting a cyber-ready and customer-centric future for banking. With platforms like BEAST, BEA is pioneering open innovation models to access expertise, test solutions and shape more secure and agile financial services. Through collaboration, BEA and partners are delivering the digital bank of tomorrow.
