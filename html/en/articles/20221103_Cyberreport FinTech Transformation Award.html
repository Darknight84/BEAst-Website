AwardBEA Wins Cyberport Award for Fintech Transformation

3 November 2022

The Bank of East Asia (BEA) received the FinTech Transformation Award at Cyberport’s FinTech Awards 2022, recognizing BEA’s leadership in digital banking through collaboration and talent development. Over the past year, BEA has worked with over 70 fintech startups and partners on 73 innovation projects.

The Cyberport FinTech Awards recognize financial institutions, startups and individuals championing the local fintech ecosystem. For BEA, the award highlights a commitment to collaboration in creating the digital bank of the future. With platforms like BEAST, research alliances and community engagement, BEA is pioneering a secure, agile and customer-centric future of digital banking. The Cyberport award reinforces BEA’s vision for collaborative transformation to advance financial services into a new digital era.
